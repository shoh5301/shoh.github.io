I am a Ph.D. candidate under the guidance of Prof. B.-J. Lee of Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH). My major field is computational materials science, and I experienced atomistic simulations for solid state materials (first-principles, MC and MD), thermodynamic calculations (CALPHAD), diffusion simulations dealing with moving boundary problems, and microstructure evolution simulations (MC Potts).
