I am a graduate student under the guidance of Professor B.-J. Lee of Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), South Korea. My major is computational materials science and engineering, especially about diffusion phenomena in multi-component alloy systems.

My recent research interest is about Nb3Sn superconducting wire, high entropy alloy, and additive manufacturing process.
